## 2. Visual Transformation

### Light (Emotional)
We removed the heavy black gradients that were suffocating the beach vibe.
*   **Why**: Sun Ninja is about *sun*. The site needed to feel like 10 AM on a clear day, not 8 PM in a storm.
*   **Execution**: Switched to a near-transparent overlay (`rgba(255,255,255,0.1)`) to let the natural light saturate the screen.

### Typography (Editorial)
*   **Why**: `DM Serif Display` was used at oversized scales to evoke editorial beach magazines (like *Kinfolk* or *Cereal*) and elevate the brand beyond e-commerce utility.
*   **Execution**: Massive 5.5rem+ headings contrast with clean `Inter` body text for effortless readability.

### Color (System)
*   **Why**: A strict palette prevents visual chaos and reinforces the "sand and sun" identity.
*   **Execution**:
    *   `--color-bg-main`: **Sand (#F7F4EF)** - The new foundation.
    *   `--color-bg-section`: **Ocean (#2F5D73)** - For depth and contrast.
    *   `--color-accent`: **Sun (#F4B400)** - Exclusive for high-priority CTAs.
